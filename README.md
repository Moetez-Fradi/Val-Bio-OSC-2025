# Val-Bio-OSC-2025
As an AI engineer, this repository documents my research, work, and conclusions during my internship at Valbio as an Orange Digital Center intern.

## About the startup

After 12 years of research in the field of plasturgy, Mr. Mohamed Medhaffer developed an innovative process to combine plastic waste and biomass into new biodegradable plastic granules. Valbio produces and sells these granules to large manufacturers that use them for molding products such as boxes, tables, chairs and more.
